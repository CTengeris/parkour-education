APK Academy At-Home Training - 65 challenges

Description

During the Covid-19 pandemic around 2020, and while the Primal Fitness parkour gym was closed, APK published a series of workout routines focused on Parkour skills and conditioning. The exercises can be performed at home with minimal equipment. Here I have collected them and curated them as a part of my collection of Parkour educational content.

Disclaimer: The video content displayed in this guide in not a replication or a redistribution of the original content. It is rather a link to the original content on the APK academy Youtube channel. All credits for the making of it go to APK and the head coach Travis Graves
